# Meeting Minutes Quality Checklist

Before final output, verify:

- The minutes are organized by topic, not just chronological transcript order.
- Decisions are labeled as decisions only when the transcript supports them.
- Action items include owner and deadline; otherwise mark `待确认`.
- Numbers, budgets, dates, names, product terms, and commitments match the transcript.
- Disagreements and unresolved questions are preserved instead of smoothed over.
- Ambiguous or low-confidence speech is marked clearly.
- Key quotes are short and materially useful.
- The summary can be understood by someone who did not attend the meeting.
- No attendee names, company names, or conclusions are invented.
